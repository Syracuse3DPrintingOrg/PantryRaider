{# Kiosk intro animation (FoodAssistant-a8xy). Included in the of every page a kiosk can land on (base.html plus the standalone start, setup, pin, and login pages). The inline guard runs synchronously before first paint: when the intro is due (kiosk mode, first load this boot), it blacks out the page so the app never flashes for a frame before the overlay appears. intro.js then plays the animation and removes the blackout; on a non-kiosk browser, or after the intro has already played this boot, the guard does nothing and intro.js exits immediately. #}
