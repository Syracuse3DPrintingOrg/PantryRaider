How it works

Create a Forager account here, then open the Pantry Raider app in your kitchen and sign in with the same email and password. That is the whole setup: scanning starts working right away.

Every account starts on a free trial. Your inventory, recipes, and shopping lists stay in your own kitchen; photos pass through Forager only long enough to be read, and are never stored.

Pantry Raider is free. Forager is the easy button.

Pantry Raider itself is free and open source. Run it at home with your own AI key and your own network setup and it works forever, no account needed. Forager is for people who would rather not: it gives your kitchen a web address you can reach from anywhere and AI scanning credits, with no keys to paste and nothing to configure.
